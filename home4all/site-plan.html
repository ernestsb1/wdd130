Overview

Purpose

The purpose of creating a children's home website is to provide information, support, and resources for children in need of a safe and nurturing environment for children's who are in need of care and protection

Audience

The intended audience for the website includes potential adoptive parents, foster families, social workers, and individuals interested in supporting and learning about the welfare of vulnerable children.
